Training

Trainings for the team are twice a week, Wednesday and Thusday

They are at ASB we will be meeting at quarter to 5 at the entrance, and we will find a free court for training start at 5. It will be a two-hour session. It will consist of a warm-up, skills and game play if we have enough people. We want to improve our skills by doing drills. But these skills can be learnt properly if they can't be put into actual game settings so during trainings we will play half court or a full game. This is to take everything we have learnt that session and put it into game play to help our brains remember it.

These trainings are very important, so it's important you make them if you can. If you are unable to please email Dave directly as he needs to know who isn't coming, so he can arrange what you guys will be doing each session. These training times should always be the same if there's any problems or training is cancelled you will receive a direct email if not there will always be updates on the notice's page on the website. That will be updated regularly so keep an eye on that.



Umpiring

In this squad we like to get volunteers

Every Week we would like one or two of you to sign up to umpire a game. You will register with your coach at training. When it comes to umpiring you need to show up 15 minutes before to sign in and find your court your on form the sign-in sheet at the reception desk at the courts. You need to do nail check and see who will start with the ball. You will be paid 15 dollars for the game you umpire. It will be really helpful to get as many of you signed up to help out and support the other teams we play against.

Fees

If you are wanting to play in a team with Daves squad it will cost you $280 for the whole season. Player fees include coach payment, registration fee and court use. If your wanting private sessions it will coast $60 for an hour price will include all the equipment used and the price of being able to use the court.
