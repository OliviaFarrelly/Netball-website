About The Coach

Dave Farrelly. He has been a netball coach for 12 years.

Coaching different ages and levels of netball. He enjoys helping people grow their netball skills and learn new ways to approach netball games. He enjoys watching people grow and improve their skills.

He likes to coach people from age 10+. He coaches some teams but also trains people individually to improve their own skills for their own netball teams. Dave is open to training people at all levels. He wants to help people who will play for his teams but also help the community by providing private lessons for girls who need a little more practice to help them reach their best potential.

He is very friendly and wants to create a good social aspect with his team because the more tight your team is the better they play. Dave is here to help teach you and your children to become the netball players they want to be.
